Welcome to studomo

We are happy to meet you here on our brandnew platform for students all over the world.

You may have noticed that we just recently started. Back in 2019, five friends, graduating from the University of Mannheim applied for different restricted master programs in various countries. Due to a lack of transparency and suitable websites, we were very insecure about how realistic our chances to succeed were. That was the moment we decided to bring this platform to life.

It is our mission to make you more confident on where to apply and at which universities your chance of getting admitted is the highest.

Beginning in April 2020, we started from scratch without any knowledge about web development. During the last few months, we worked hard to get to our current state and we are dedicated to improve our website further and further to offer you the best experience. We hope you are happy with our idea and appreciate suggestions of any kind. Feel free to contact us.

And that's us...
