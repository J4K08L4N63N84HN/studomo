About studomo

Studomo is a platform for students from all over the world to better assess their chances for a successful university application. Our goal is to provide a transparent and anonymized database which offers you CV-like profiles with students that have either been accepted or rejected to a specific program.

This database contains over 3000 universities from all over the world with around 50 different disciplines. Each entry in our database is displayed in a structured way, so you will be able to see which university the applicant has attended, his/her work experience or what his/her results were in tests like the GMAT or GRE. This way, you will find precise information about the applicantâ€™s background and whether the application was successful.

By means of that you can check whether your profile is sufficient for your pursued studies by comparing yourself to other applicants. You may realize that you are an optimal candidate for a very competitive MBA program in a top business school or for a prestigious international program at universities like Oxford, Stanford or Harvard.

We wish you all the best for a successful application process!
